Replacing the Ink

Replace spent ink cartridges.

When the pen runs out of ink, follow these steps to replace the pen's ink cartridge.
  1. Get a replacement ink cartridge. If you don't have one, contact the SDC Technology Team (sdc-tech@ucdavis.edu).
  2. Pull the old ink cartridge out of the pen. This may take a few tries.
  3. Gently push the new ink cartridge into the pen until you feel a little resistance and the cartridge stops moving.
Now, you can keep writing.
