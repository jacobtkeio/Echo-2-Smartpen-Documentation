Deleting a Recording

Delete a recording to clear up storage space.

When the pen's storage is full, follow these steps to delete a recording and free up space using the menu (fig. 4).
Note: You must use the pen's menu. You cannot delete recordings based on notebook pages.
  1. On the Control Panel's "Menu" section, tap the center "MENU" icon with the writing tip of the pen (see fig. 3).
  2. Tap the "down" icon.
    The screen will say, "Paper Replay."
  3. Tap the "right" icon.
    The screen will say, "Record new session."
  4. Tap the "down" icon two times.
    The screen will say, "Play Session," and then "Delete Session."
  5. Tap the "right" icon.
    The screen will show the date of your last recording.
  6. To select which recording you will delete, tap the "up" and "down" icons. Recordings are named by the date and time they were recorded.
  7. To delete the selected recording, tap the "right" icon.
  8. (Optional) To quickly return to the home screen, tap the pen on any surface that isn't an icon.
You have deleted a recording and made space for new recordings.
